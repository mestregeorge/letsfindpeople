This Cookies Policy explains how LetsFindPeople uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work, improve performance, and provide information to the site owner.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (always active)
These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication cookies: keep you logged in during your session so you do not need to re-enter your credentials on every page.
  • Security cookies: help detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorised automated access.
  • Session cookies: maintain your session state (e.g. your current search or profile edit in progress).

Functional Cookies (optional)
These cookies remember your preferences to improve your experience, such as your chosen language or display settings. We only set these with your consent.

Analytics Cookies (optional)
With your consent, we may use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with the Service — for example, which keywords are searched most, how users navigate the site, and where users drop off in the registration flow. This data is collected in aggregate and pseudonymous form and is used solely to improve the Service. No analytics cookies are set until you provide consent.

3. We Do Not Use Advertising Cookies

We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies, and we do not share your browsing activity with advertising networks.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent the Service from functioning correctly. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies currently stored.
  • Delete all or selected cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites.

For guidance, visit your browser's help documentation.

We may update this Cookies Policy as our use of cookies changes. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
